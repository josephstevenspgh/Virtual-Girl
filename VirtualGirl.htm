Controls


Arrow Keys or WASD to move
Up, W, space, or X to jump
Press Down to enter doors

Abilities

Double Jump allows you to jump... twice.
Blink gives you the ability to click anywhere within QT-Pi's vision, to instantly teleport! You can only use this once every 30 seconds.
Liquid Proof allows you to enter otherwise hazardous substances, like water. Or even lava.

Background


This game was made within 48 hours for Ludum Dare 21. This was my second 48-hour game, and was a lot of fun to make. Of course, I didn't get to make it as good as I wanted to, but I still really enjoyed the process. As always, it feels great to finish something.
